So, who am I?

You might call me YOKS, but my name is Oluwasemilore Olarogun.

My strong passions are serving the hungry and the destitute, and leading youngsters to Christ.

I am particularly interested in seeing young children develop into fervent followers of the Lord,

Who advance the goals of the Kingdom.

In addition to all of this, I am a biochemist, an HR specialist,

And a product designer who is currently studying to become a Web developer

I value my faith greatly and I don't take my spiritual life lightly

I enjoy encouraging others and trying to be of the most assistance to them.
